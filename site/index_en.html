Log book

    To track day to day activities of your teams and people. Just to records answers on simple questions:

  • What did you do yesterday?
  • What will you do today?
  • How much time spent on issue?
  • Are there any impediments in the way?

Open Log book

Features

    Create account, organize people and teams. Track activities. Tracking daily activities possible using keyboard and/or audio recordings. No special tools are required for audio logging - just web browser. Get various reports, based on recorded information:

  • on daily activities
  • on a personâ€™s activities
  • activity for a period, on tasks
  • etc.
