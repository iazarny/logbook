Details.

    A simple application designed to track people's activity. This application is not coupled with any methodology. Audio recording of activity can also be used.

    After account registering and adding people to it, you can - organize teams for convenience. Track and record people's daily activities using keyboard and audio recordings. You can record audio from the site. Participants can also record their activities on their own. No specifal tools is required - just web browser.

    An activity record is a simple set of

  • task number
  • brief overview of the task and what has been done
  • how much time has been spent
  • a sign of completion of the task.
For example: LBA-027, screwed a wing to the aircraft fuselage. Spent 6h 30m. Mission accomplished.

    Based on the registered activity, you can receive various reports:

  • on daily activities
  • on a person’s activities
  • activity for a period, on tasks
  • etc.

    Actually this log book just a tracking of daily scrum meeting in online and based on scrum meeting ceremony. It helps focus the entire team on sprint goals showing who does what. And it doesn’t matter if the team is office or remote. Here is a way to run them efficiently.

  1. Each memeber can choose a schedule that works for him and report online of offline on meeting.
  2. Scrum master can track activity of the team online.
  3. During the meeting, scrum master will ask everyone three simple questions.
    • what did you do yesterday?
    • what do you plan to do today?
    • Do you have any obstacles?

    Need to remember main goal - is to let team members talk about how team goals are progressing. Therefore, do not need to ask each person to report their status? just track it.
