Features

    A simple application designed to track people's activity. This application is not coupled with any methodology.

    After account registering and adding people to it, you can - organize teams for convenience. Track and record people's daily activities using keyboard and audio recordings. You can record audio from the site. Participants can also record their activities on their own. No specifal tools is required - just web browser.

    An activity record is a simple set of

  • task number
  • brief overview of the task and what has been done
  • how much time has been spent
  • a sign of completion of the task.
For example: LBA-027, screwed a wing to the aircraft fuselage. Spent 6h 30m. Mission accomplished.
